list_alt

PO List

View all purchase orders with their status (Draft, Pending, Approved, Rejected, Delivered). Filter by status or search by PO number / supplier name. Each row shows items count, total value and priority.

add_shopping_cart

Create PO

Select a supplier, set priority (Normal / High / Urgent), add line items with quantity and unit price. The total is calculated automatically. Use Save as Draft to save for later or Submit for Approval to send it to the approver.

rule

Approvals

Lists all POs pending your approval. Review the supplier, value and priority, then click Approve or Reject. Approved POs move to the active procurement pipeline.

receipt

Invoice Mapping

Match received supplier invoices to their corresponding POs. Auto-matched invoices are highlighted in green. Use Match Manually for unmatched invoices to reconcile your payables accurately.

lightbulb Tip: Set priority to Urgent only for critical stockouts — it helps approvers triage faster and avoids approval queue bottlenecks.
