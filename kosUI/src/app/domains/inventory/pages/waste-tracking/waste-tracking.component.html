info_outline How to use Waste Tracking
add_circle_outline

Waste Entry

Log every waste incident in real-time. Fill in the item name, category, quantity, reason and estimated cost, then click Record Waste. Recent entries appear in the table below the form.

list_alt

Reports

View the complete waste log filtered by Today / Week / Month / Year. Each record shows date, item, category, quantity, reason and cost — useful for audits and kitchen reviews.

bar_chart

Analytics

Bar charts break waste down by Category (Food, Beverage, Packaging) and Reason (Expired, Overcooked, Damaged…). Use this to identify the biggest loss drivers and take corrective action.

trending_down

Trends

The weekly summary table compares each week's total waste cost against the previous week (green = reduction ✓, red = increase ✗). The daily line chart shows this week's cost curve at a glance.

lightbulb Tip: Record waste immediately after it occurs for the most accurate cost tracking. Even small entries add up — consistent logging helps reduce weekly waste by up to 20%.
