info_outline How to use Supplier Management
list_alt

Supplier List

View all registered suppliers with their contact details, status and star rating. Filter by Active / Inactive / Pending or search by name. Click a row to view full details.

person_add

Add / Edit Supplier

Fill in the supplier form (name, contact, email, category, payment terms) and click Save Supplier to register a new one. To edit, select an existing supplier from the list first — the form pre-fills automatically.

insights

Supplier Performance

Compare suppliers by on-time delivery %, quality rating and average delivery days. Use this to identify your most reliable vendors and renegotiate terms with under-performers.

receipt_long

Transactions History

Full invoice and payment history per supplier. Status badges show Paid, Pending or Overdue. Use this for reconciliation and payment follow-ups.

lightbulb Tip: Regularly review the Performance tab — suppliers with on-time delivery below 80% should be flagged for review to prevent stockouts.
