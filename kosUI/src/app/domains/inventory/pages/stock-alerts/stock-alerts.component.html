Stock Alerts

Monitor inventory levels, expiry dates and critical stock conditions

info_outline How to use Stock Alerts
dashboard

Dashboard

Overview of all active alerts — total, critical, low stock and expiring soon. Insight cards highlight the most urgent conditions at a glance.

inventory_2

Low Stock

Lists items that have dropped below their minimum threshold. Filter by category or search by name. Click Reorder to initiate a purchase order for the item.

event_busy

Expiry Alerts

Shows batches nearing their expiry date. Days remaining are colour-coded: red ≤ 3 days, amber ≤ 7 days, green otherwise. Act before items expire to minimise waste.

tune

Settings

Configure the thresholds that trigger alerts (low stock units, critical units, expiry warning days) and toggle email or SMS notifications. Click Save Settings to apply.

lightbulb Tip: Set your critical threshold to at least 2× your average daily usage so you have enough lead time to reorder before running out.
